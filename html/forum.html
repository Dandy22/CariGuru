About

As someone who comes from a non-computer science background, I understand the doubts that can come with making a career switch. However, I'm excited to share my personal experience of successfully transitioning into the tech industry, particularly within the fintech sector. Over the past five years, I have gained valuable experience working in a variety of environments, from small and agile teams to large-scale projects that serve millions of users. Through these experiences, I've gained a deep understanding of the industry and the skills required to succeed in it. I am passionate about sharing my knowledge and experiences with others.
